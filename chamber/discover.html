Heritage Park

Park offering a sports field, a picnic shelter, walking trails & playgrounds for big & little kids.

Jimi Hendrix Memorial

Elaborate memorial dedicated to guitarist Jimi Hendrix in the Greenwood Memorial Park cemetery.

350 Monroe Ave NE, Renton, WA 98056

Renton Carco Theatre

A 300-seat auditorium within Cedar River Park, hosting independent films, children's programs, and multicultural events.

1717 SE Maple Valley Hwy, Renton, WA 98057

Virginia Mason Athletic Center

The headquarters and practice facility of the NFL's Seattle Seahawks, offering public training camp sessions.

12 Seahawks Way, Renton, WA 98056

Northwest Seaplanes

Offers Seattle tour flights and charter services to the San Juan Islands and British Columbia's Inside Passage.

860 W Perimeter Rd, Renton, WA 98057

Gene Coulon Memorial Beach Park

A 57-acre park along Lake Washington featuring a swimming beach, boat launch, walking trails, and dining options.

1201 Lake Washington Blvd N, Renton, WA 98056

Renton Public Library

Uniquely built over the Cedar River, this library offers scenic views and a vast collection of resources.

100 Mill Ave S, Renton, WA 98057

A professional performing arts center offering a variety of productions in a 1920s movie palace setting.

507 S 3rd St, Renton, WA 98057
