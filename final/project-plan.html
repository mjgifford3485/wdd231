Site Plan Proposal

Site Name:

Tabletop RPG Adventures!

The reason for this title is that it includes the topic of the site, which is tabletop rpgs, and couples it with an action word, adventures, to give potential visitors a sense of excitement.

Domain Name: tabletoprgpadventures.com

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to provide information about tabletop roleplaying games, including resources, necessary items, and types of games.

Scenarios:

What are tabletop roleplaying games?

What are some different types of roleplaying games availabe?

What tools do I need to play?

How can I find others to play?
